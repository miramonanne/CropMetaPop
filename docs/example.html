Selfing

This example allows to show the rate of heterozygote of a marker in term of type of mating.

selfing.txt 

 1 generations:50
 2
 3 carr_capacity:100
 4
 5 selfing:0.2
 6 #selfing:0.5
 7 #selfing:0.8
 8 #selfing:1
